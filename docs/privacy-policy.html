Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026

WallBreaker is a Telegram Mini App. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use WallBreaker.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following data:

  • Telegram account identifiers, such as your Telegram user ID, username, and profile name, if provided by Telegram.
  • In-app activity data, such as taps, rewards, rank progress, referrals, draw participation, and other gameplay-related actions.
  • Transaction data related to in-app purchases and rewards, including Telegram Stars payments and Toncoin payment verification data when applicable.
  • Technical data required for security and service operation, such as IP address, device/browser information, and basic request logs.

2. How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

  • provide and operate WallBreaker features;
  • process gameplay progress, rewards, referrals, and draw participation;
  • verify payments and prevent fraud or abuse;
  • maintain security, stability, and performance of the service;
  • comply with legal obligations, if required.

3. Payments

WallBreaker may support payments through Telegram Stars and Toncoin-related payment flows. We do not store your private wallet keys or full card details. We only process the data necessary to verify and record transactions.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data only with infrastructure, hosting, analytics, payment, or anti-fraud services strictly when necessary to operate and secure WallBreaker.

5. Data Retention

We keep data only for as long as it is reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain security, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children

WallBreaker is not intended for users who are prohibited from using such services under applicable law. If you believe data was provided unlawfully, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of your data. To make a request, contact us by email.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of WallBreaker after changes means you accept the updated version.
